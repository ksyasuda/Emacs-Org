#+TITLE: Cnf Resolution
#+STARTUP: inlineimages

* CNF
** A v something ^ B V something
** it is the conjunction of or clauses
** A is in CNF since A v F ^ T or A v T ^ F
** A v B is CNF since (A V B) ^ T
* convert to CNF
* A -> B
** -A V B
* Vx A(x)->B(x)
** Vx -A(x) V B(x)
** drop the universal quantifier
** -A(x) V B(x)
